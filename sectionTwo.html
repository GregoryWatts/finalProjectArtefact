Methods

To make coffee there are many ways to make it 2 are mentioned here.

One Method is through a french press also known as a cafetiere. The way it works is that the coffee grounds are placed into the press a big container essentially and then hot water is then mixed with it. After around 5 minutes its ready to be poured through a filter into a mug.

Second Method is a pour over system. It is when coffee grounds are placed in a metal or paper filter. Then water is poured over it for it to filter the coffee through to then be poured into a mug to drink.

Benefits of each method.
Method 1: A stronger cup can be made as the grounds are exposed to the water more.
Method 1: It does not require repurchase of coffee filters
Method 2: A smoother cup can be made from it making it less bitter.
Method 2: You can make multiple cups much more easily through this method.
